The Jordan/Boseman matter

  • “Our firm represents Michael P. Jordan. Jordan was injured while visiting an estate owned by Chad Boseman in Wakanda, Connecticut.”
  • “Jordan wants to know if it even makes sense to sue Boseman for his injuries or whether Boseman has landowner immunity.”
  • “[Y]ou should not address that exception to immunity [for failure to warn] in your research. In addition, do not consider any trespass issues. Limit your analysis to whether Boseman has landowner immunity from liability.”

Research Memo Assignment

How do we approach an open-ended assignment?

General Approaches

  • Understand the facts
  • Understand what the client wants to know
  • Learn the law
  • Identify the legal issue
  • Classify and organize the facts pursuant to the law
  • Identify best case law
