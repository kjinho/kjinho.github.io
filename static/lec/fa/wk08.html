The Dollar Depot matter

  • “Our firm represents Dollar Depot, Inc., which is being sued by a customer for false imprisonment.”

  • “On Wednesday, July 13, 2025, at approximately 10:00 am, Francine Groner entered the Houston Dollar Depot ….”

  • “On that day, Smith was overseeing the cashiers and helping customers. She was also on the lookout for potential shoplifters.”

  • Smith stopped and searched Groner on suspicion of shoplifting. She determined Groner was not shoplifting.

  • “Groner is now suing Dollar Depot for false imprisonment. Another associate is researching the viability of Groner’s false imprisonment claim. Please research whether Dollar Depot has a defense to Groner’s claim.”

How do we approach an open-ended assignment?

A. General Approaches

  • Understand the facts

  • Understand what the client wants to know

  • Learn the law

  • Identify the legal issue

C. Dismissal and Summary Judgment

  • Dismissal for failure to state a claim: The allegations, taken as true, do not entitle the claimant to the relief sought. See Tex. R. Civ. P. 91a.1; Fed. R. Civ. P. 12(b)(6).

  • Summary judgment: There is no genuine issue as to any material fact and the moving party is entitled to judgment as a matter of law. See Tex. R. Civ. P. 166a(c); Fed. R. Civ. P. 56(a).

  • What does it mean for the case-law facts if a court denies a motion for summary judgment?

D. How do we classify and organize the facts?

  • Start with the law: shopkeeper defenses for false imprisonment

  • Tex. Civ. Prac. & Rem. Code Ann. § 124.001(a) (West 2023) (“A person who reasonably believes that another has stolen or is attempting to steal property is privileged to detain that person in a reasonable manner and for a reasonable time to investigate ownership of the property.”).

  • Wal-Mart Stores, Inc. v. Resendez, 962 S.W.2d 539, 540 (Tex. 1998).

  • Elements:

    • “reasonably believes that another has stolen or is attempting to steal property”

    • “detain that person in a reasonable manner”

    • “for a reasonable time”
