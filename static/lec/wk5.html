The Dollar Depot matter

  • “Our firm represents Dollar Depot, Inc., which is being sued by a customer for false imprisonment.”
  • “On Wednesday, July 13, 2022, at approximately 10:00 am, Francine Groner entered the Houston Dollar Depot ....”
  • “Sandra Smith, the store manager on duty at the time, was standing at the front of the store.”
  • “On that day, Smith was overseeing the cashiers and helping customers. She was also on the lookout for potential shoplifters.”
  • Smith stopped and searched Groner on suspicion of shoplifting. She determined Groner was not shoplifting.
  • “Groner is now suing Dollar Depot for false imprisonment. Another associate is researching the viability of Groner’s false imprisonment claim. Please research whether Dollar Depot has a defense to Groner’s claim.”

Research Memo Assignment

How do we approach an open-ended assignment?

General Approaches

  • Understand the facts
  • Understand what the client wants to know
  • Learn the law
  • Identify the legal issue
  • Classify and organize the facts pursuant to the law
  • Identify best case law

What makes a good (predictive) case?

  • Court
  • Legal issue
  • Facts
  • Recency

How do we classify and organize the facts?

  • Start from the law governing shopkeeper defenses for false imprisonment
  • Tex. Civ. Prac. & Rem. Code Ann. § 124.001 (West 1985)
  • Wal-Mart Stores, Inc. v. Resendez, 962 S.W.2d 539, 540 (Tex. 1998)
  • Elements
    • “reasonably believes that another has stolen or is attempting to steal property”
    • “detain that person in a reasonable manner”
    • “for a reasonable time”
